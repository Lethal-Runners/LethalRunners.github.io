Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for the Organization Lethal Runners

Last updated: September 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how our VR app collects, uses, and protects your data.

Information We Collect

    User-generated content (e.g., usernames, chat messages)

    Device and usage data (e.g., headset model, crash logs)

    Interaction data (e.g., in-app behavior, hand tracking, microphone input if applicable)

    Age of users accessing the app

How We Use Your Data

    To improve app functionality and user experience

    To analyze usage and performance

    To ensure user safety and prevent abuse

    To ensure that the game is suitable for the intended age range

Data Storage and Security

We store data securely using encryption where appropriate. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.

Sharing of Data

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with in-game moderators for analytics and crash reporting purposes.

We only share user data with PlayFab to provide item management services.

Disposal of Data

Users can request that their game data be deleted, clearing all history except ban history to prevent abuse of the system.

Data will be automatically deleted after a period of 3 months of inactivity, including ban data.

Requesting Permission to Use Assets
