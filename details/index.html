Game Details

About the Game

Lethal Runners is a fast-paced VR parkour game where you can climb trees, swing through obstacles, and collect hidden pink crystals. The game is built with passion for the VR community and offers a unique experience for players who love movement and exploration.

This game is about hanging out with friends and socializing in a virtual environment. Meet amazing moderators, unlock exclusive cosmetics, and enjoy the freedom of movement in a beautifully crafted world.

Key Features

  • Fast-paced VR parkour mechanics
  • Climb trees and swing through challenging obstacles
  • Collect hidden pink crystals throughout the map
  • Unlock exclusive cosmetics and customization options
  • Social experience with friends and community moderators
  • Immersive forest environment designed for exploration
